For my undergraduate thesis project I worked on detecting gravitational waves through the use of convolutional neural networks. I first started by attempting to recreate the signal data using [PyCBC](https://pycbc.org/) and then creating data for my convolutional neural network. Unfortunately, the model failed to accurately detect signals in the gravitational wave data. The repository can be found [here](https://github.com/DarryenSands/Thesis-Project-2021).
